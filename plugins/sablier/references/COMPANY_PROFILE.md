## Products

### Sablier Lockup

Token vesting and distribution with customizable unlock schedules.

**Use cases**: Team vesting, Investor allocations, Advisor tokens, Grants

**Key features:**

- Linear, cliff, or custom unlock curves
- Cancelable or non-cancelable streams
- NFT representation of streams (tradeable)
- Snapshot governance integration
- Safe multisig integration

### Sablier Airdrops

Vested airdrops that prevent day-1 dumping.

**Use cases**: Token launches, Community rewards, Retroactive distributions

**Key features:**

- Up to 50,000 recipients per campaign
- Merkle-tree based (gas efficient)
- Recipients pay claim gas (not creator)
- Optional expiration dates
- Recoverable unclaimed tokens

**Differentiator**: Traditional airdrops release all tokens at once, leading to immediate selling. Sablier Airdrops
vest tokens over time, aligning community incentives.

### Sablier Flow

Open-ended payment streaming for recurring payments.

**Use cases**: Payroll, Subscriptions, Continuous grants

**Key features:**

- No fixed end date
- Adjustable flow rates
- Pause and resume
- Real-time withdrawals

**Status**: Launched December 2024

## Key Concepts

### Money Streaming

**Definition**: Distributing assets on a continuous, by-the-second basis rather than in lump sums

**Why it matters**: Aligns incentives, provides real-time access, enables new financial primitives

**Coined by**: Sablier (2019)

### Sablier Airdrops

**Definition**: Vested airdrops where recipients receive tokens over time rather than all at once

**Why it matters**: Prevents day-1 dumping, creates long-term community alignment

### Stream NFTs

**Definition**: Each Sablier stream is represented as an NFT that can be traded, used in governance, etc.

**Why it matters**: Enables liquidity for locked tokens, composability with other protocols
